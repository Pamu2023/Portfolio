About the Project

ShopHub is a premium e-commerce web app designed to create a smooth shopping journey from product browsing to checkout. It solves the problem of crowded and confusing shopping interfaces by using clear navigation, product filters, detailed product pages, wishlist support, simple cart management, and a step-by-step secure checkout flow.
