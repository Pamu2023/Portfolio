About the Project

Imili Pocket is a personal finance tracker app designed to help users manage income, expenses, and monthly budgets through a simple analytics dashboard. The app solves the problem of unclear money tracking by using summary cards, category spending charts, income trends, and budget comparison visuals, allowing users to understand their financial habits and make better spending decisions.

Tested the Imili Pocket prototype with 5 users to evaluate dashboard readability, transaction entry, and budget tracking. Based on feedback, improved 3 flows to make financial summaries, transaction actions, and budget progress easier to understand.
