About the Project

Brew is a mobile coffee shop app designed to make online coffee ordering simple, fast, and visually appealing. The project focuses on solving the problem of confusing and crowded ordering interfaces by creating a clean user flow from login and product browsing to coffee customization, payment, and order confirmation. Using warm coffee-inspired colors, clear product cards, simple navigation, and easy customization options, the app helps users quickly find their favorite coffee, personalize it, and complete their order smoothly.

Tested the Brew prototype with 5 users to evaluate the coffee ordering flow, drink customization, and checkout process. Based on feedback, refined 3 screens to improve navigation clarity, product selection, and payment flow.

About the Project

Imili Pocket is a personal finance tracker app designed to help users manage income, expenses, and monthly budgets through a simple analytics dashboard. The app solves the problem of unclear money tracking by using summary cards, category spending charts, income trends, and budget comparison visuals, allowing users to understand their financial habits and make better spending decisions.

Tested the Imili Pocket prototype with 5 users to evaluate dashboard readability, transaction entry, and budget tracking. Based on feedback, improved 3 flows to make financial summaries, transaction actions, and budget progress easier to understand.
